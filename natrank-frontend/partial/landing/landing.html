Hi!

Welcome! This website provides Elo ratings of national football teams using match data spanning from 30 November 1872, when the first international football match took place, up to this day (not yet!).

We are in beta...

The website is in early beta stage (it does not even have a proper name yet!). Currently, we have match data available from 30/11/1872 to {{latestMatchDate}}. The missing match data will be added in the near future, along with many new features and bug-fixes.

...but we are launching soon!

We are planning to launch in early 2015. Meanwhile you can check out some websites from which we took inspiration: clubelo.com and eloratings.net.
